ABOUT US

WELCOME TO
PIONEER COACHING

Pioneer (Head Office) provides coaching for competitive examinations for Government services and entrance test. Like SSC, BIHAR POLICE, BANKS, RAILWAY, POLICE SERVICES, STATE SERVICES,CENTERAL SCHOOLS, ARMY,PARA MILITARY FORCES.

Established in 2010 and registered under Bihar Directorate of Education. We ensure that the learning needs of the young generation are adequately met to bring excellence and professionalism in education.

One of the primary goals of coaching is to teach children at minimum fees so that even the poorest of the poor children can complete their studies and their parents do not have to face any kind of pain and difficulties.

The coaching has received excellent reviews on the personality and quality as it keeps motivating us to serve the society with our best interests in mind.

MISSION

Education has always been the critical factor ushering in enlightenment to a society. The need for progress has necessitated the evolution of learning from the stagnancy of passive thinking to a quest for greater learning and pushing for higher feats achievable in our human capacity. Percieving the vital role of education in holistic growth, our motto "Learning with a vision" expresses the vision of maximizing our ability as individuals to contribute to the welfare of all. Pioneer Institute believes in imparting genuine knowledge and right guidance to every student.

VISION

To supplement the competent workforce of our progressive nation, the need for employing high caliber individuals has never been more pressing. It has propelled the benchmark for educational qualification to heights attainable only with exceptional direction and mentoring. We are, therefore, wholly committed to imparting career-based education. We have designed our courses to effectively meet the requirements of young men and women in search of proper guidance and coaching- to achieve their dreams and aspirations.

APPROACH

A rich blend of well qualified and experienced faculty is always on the clock, working to keeping up with the pace of the fast-changing and competitive environment. Meticulously planned courses, timely completion of the syllabus and consistent periodic tests to adapt to the paper pattern- ushers to comprehensively equip the aspirants.
